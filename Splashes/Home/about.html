Our Story

Founded in 2010, Splashes Swim School started with a vision to make water safety accessible and enjoyable. We've grown from a single pool to three locations, all focused on safe, structured instruction.

Meet the Instructors

  • Coach Mike: Lifeguard trainer and competitive swim coach.
  • Coach Lily: Specialist in adaptive aquatic programs for all abilities.
